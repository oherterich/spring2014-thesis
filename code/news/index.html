Past & Present at the MFADT Thesis Show

May 10 2014

Past & Present will be shown at the MFA Design & Technology show at Parsons School of Design. Join us this between May 17 and May 25 for an exhibition of 70+ projects by emerging artists, designers, coders, makers, hackers, educators, and thinkers from Parsons' MFA Design and Technology program.
